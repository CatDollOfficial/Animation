Guidelines

A quick guide so players always know what to do and what each action means.

  1. Pet the cat to increase the pet count and earn progress. Twenty-five pets are enough to reach the next level by petting alone.
  2. Feed her from the bowl to refill hunger and give her a little extra XP boost.
  3. If hunger gets low, her face turns sad, so keep an eye on the hunger bar.
  4. Night Mode is for resting and cozy vibes, and hunger pauses while she is sleeping.
  5. At level 50, the rainbow celebration appears after a short 5-second hero moment.
  6. Use Reset Game anytime if you want to start fresh from level 1.
